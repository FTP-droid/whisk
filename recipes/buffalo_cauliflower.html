Spicy Buffalo Cauliflower Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (like Frank's RedHot)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cut cauliflower into bite-sized florets and pat dry.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together hot sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  4. Add cauliflower florets to the bowl and toss until well coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Spread cauliflower in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until edges are crispy and golden brown.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired. Serve immediately with celery sticks or vegan ranch.
