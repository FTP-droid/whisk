Traditional Eggplant Caponata

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons capers
  • 1/2 cup green olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© chopped eggplant until golden brown, about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Add onion and celery, cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in capers, olives, and diced tomatoes.
  5. Add red wine vinegar and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes until vegetables are soft and flavors are blended.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature. Can be stored in refrigerator for up to a week.
