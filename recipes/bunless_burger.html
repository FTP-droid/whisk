Bunless Burger with Avocado

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 4-6 large butter lettuce leaves
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: garlic powder, onion powder

Instructions

  1. Season ground beef with salt, pepper, and optional spices. Form into 4 patties.
  2. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  3. Grill beef patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. While patties cook, wash and dry lettuce leaves, slice tomato, onion, and avocado.
  5. Place each cooked patty on large lettuce leaves.
  6. Top with tomato slices, onion, and avocado.
  7. Wrap in lettuce leaves and serve immediately.
