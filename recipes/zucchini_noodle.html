Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

Ingredients

  • 3 medium zucchini
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (or pine nuts)
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Spiralize zucchini into noodles using a spiralizer or vegetable peeler.
  2. In a food processor, blend basil, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, walnuts, and nutritional yeast until smooth.
  3. Season pesto with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss the pesto with zucchini noodles until well coated.
  5. Serve immediately cold or slightly warmed. Enjoy!
