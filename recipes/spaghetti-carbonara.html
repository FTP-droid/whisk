Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta, mix with pancetta, then stir in egg mixture off the heat.
  5. Season and serve immediately.
